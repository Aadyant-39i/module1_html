Gopalan international school

Gopalan international school is a co-educational day school located in Bangalore, India. It was established in 2002 and is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The school offers education from kindergarten to grade 12 and has a strong focus on academics, sports, and extracurricular activities. The school's mission is to provide a nurturing and inclusive environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and global citizenship among its students.
