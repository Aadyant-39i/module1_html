Facts about spacefish

Spacefish are a unique, distinct type of fish that live in space. They need water to survive, and they live inside melted polar caps of planets, or clumps of water floating in space.

Did you know?

Spacefish can survive in the vacuum of space for short periods of time.It can survive in vaccuum for 2 minutes to even 12 hours.
 Spacefish have a special adaptation that allows them to extract oxygen from the water they live in. Their life expectancy is quite long, often living for several decades.
 Spacefish primarily live in the icy regions of mars and the moons of jupiter, where they have adapted to the harsh conditions.
