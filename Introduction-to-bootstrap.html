Maserati is a historic Italian luxury car manufacturer founded on December 1, 1914, in Bologna, renowned for its racing heritage, distinctive exhaust notes, and the iconic Trident emblem. Now headquartered in Modena and owned by Stellantis, it competes with brands like Aston Martin and Porsche, offering high-performance vehicles like the MC20, GranTurismo, and SUVs.

The Lockheed Martin F-22 Raptor is a fifth-generation, single-seat, twin-engine stealth fighter designed primarily for air superiority, though it also has ground-attack capabilities. It is renowned for its unmatched maneuverability, stealth technology, and "supercruise" speed (exceeding Mach 1.5 without afterburners). With 185-195 produced, it is exclusively operated by the U.S. Air Force, with production having ceased in 2011.
